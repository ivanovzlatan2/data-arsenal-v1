# Example Store - Business Context

## Business Context

### Goals
- Monthly revenue target: 100,000 EUR
- Quarterly growth target: 15% vs last year
- Key metric: Revenue per session
- Secondary: New customer acquisition rate

### Funnel
- Main channels: Google Ads (40%), Organic Search (25%), Meta Ads (20%), Email (15%)
- Expected behavior: Category page -> Product page -> Add to cart -> Checkout
- Average conversion rate: 2.3%
- Average order value: 85 EUR
- Key drop-off: Cart to checkout (60% abandonment)

### Returning Clients
- 35% of revenue from returning customers
- Email flows: post-purchase, cart abandonment, win-back
- Typical repurchase: 45 days

### Seasonality
- Peak: November-December (Black Friday, Christmas)
- Low: January-February
- Regular promotions: Monthly flash sales (10-15% lift)
- Summer sale in July

### Competitors
- Competitor A: Stronger brand, higher prices, better mobile experience
- Competitor B: Lower prices, wider catalog, weaker customer service
- Competitor C: Niche player, dominates organic search in our top category

### Differentiator
- Curated selection with expert reviews
- Same-day shipping in capital city
- Strongest product category: home electronics (45% of revenue)
