# Business Context Template

Fill in what you know. Skip what you don't. Partial context is infinitely better than no context. Update the "What Changed" section weekly or whenever something happens.

### 6. Our Differentiator
- Why do customers choose us over alternatives? ___
- Our strongest product/service: ___
- Our weakest area: ___
